Digital Export
what is all about?

The digital transformation of export sales has created new skills and a new job position: the Digital Export Manager. The Digital Exporter Series is the most complete and up-to-date collection of books offering immediately actionable solutions to any export manager or exporting business that wants to grow B2B and B2C sales online on the international scale. It consists of seven independent books, each on a different topic, with more than 100 case studies that describe, step by step, all the web tools and techniques for cross-border digital sales. If you want to launch your business in digital export, and you want to know how the internet and web marketing work in foreign countries, you are in the right place. But, before getting started, let me tell you a big secret... Most of what you can find online about international web marketing is not useful! If you've searched on Google for ‘international web marketing’ or ‘international digital strategy’, you may have noticed three things:
  1. The content talks mainly about how to help local businesses go abroad but says little or nothing about helping foreign companies do so. As a result, the understanding and explanations of technical web features and online habits in foreign countries are limited.
  2. The content very often focuses on one or very few specific web tasks. For instance, only on international Google Ads, or only on multilingual website development, etc. As a result, it is not effective when it comes to helping companies design a holistic digital export strategy.
  3. Finally, the same content usually deals with digital skills and doesn’t address export/import issues, such as how to manage VAT, cross-border fulfilment, international payments, etc. Once again, this kind of content is not effective when it comes to helping companies design a comprehensive digital export strategy.
Selling online abroad is very different from selling online in your own country. Problems and mistakes with web techniques applied internationally can cause serious damage to your online performance that can take a long time to recover from. But you can easily avoid them when you have the right knowledge. No matter your background, the mission of The Digital Exporter Series is to provide you all the explanations, tips, and tools to start, manage and boost your digital export.   1. Book 1 – STARTING THE GLOBAL DIGITAL JOURNEY: How to create the best international web strategy.
  2. Book 2 – GETTING CLIENTS OVERSEAS, ORGANICALLY: How to set up local, international & global SEO.
  3. Book 3 – GETTING CLIENTS OVERSEAS, BY ADVERTISING: SEA, SMA and other acquisition channels.
  4. Book 4 – SELLING ONLINE INTERNATIONALLY: How to set up & manage a cross-border e-commerce.
  5. Book 5 – SELLING ONLINE INTERNATIONALLY: How to sell on local & international marketplaces.

  7. Book 7 – DEVELOPING A STRONG INTERNATIONAL BRAND: Multilingual inbound marketing & marketing automation.
